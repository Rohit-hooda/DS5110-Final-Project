View real-time weather data, track trends, and analyze historical patterns all in one intuitive dashboard.

Current Weather Across Massachusetts Counties

Hover over the boundaries to view the county name, and click on the shapes for detailed weather attributes such as temperature, humidity, and precipitation.

Temperature Heatmap: This map highlights the maximum temperatures recorded across Massachusetts counties. Warmer colors represent higher temperatures.

Minimum Temperature Heatmap: This map highlights the minimum temperatures recorded across Massachusetts counties. Cooler colors represent lower temperatures.

Precipitation Heatmap: This map shows the probability of precipitation across Massachusetts counties. Darker areas indicate higher precipitation probabilities.

UV Index Heatmap: This map illustrates maximum UV Index levels across Massachusetts counties. Higher values indicate more intense UV radiation.

Wind Gusts Heatmap: This map highlights the maximum wind gusts recorded across Massachusetts counties. Areas with darker shades represent stronger gusts.
